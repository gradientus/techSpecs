Introduction

DIY wine making is very simple. Yeast, grapes, sugar ferment to make wine. Wine is one of the most ancient beverages. It was discovered before written history, thousands of years ago. Wild yeast settled on rotting grapes which were pooling in the shade on a rock. A human tasted it, and humanity has never been the same. Imagine a dinner without wine. How dare you!

Wine making has been refined over the ages. DIY winemaking lies between control and chaos, science and art. With a bit of luck, the right equipment, quality ingredients, and time we will create wine at home.Let's try it now!
